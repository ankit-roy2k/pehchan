Pehchan E-Learning



Automated Attendance System


An automated, accurate, fast, modern and a no-touch approach of marking attendance.

Click Here

‘Pehchan E- Learning’ as the name suggests, is used to recognise single or multiple faces at an instance of time in a closed environment. This technology can be used at colleges and offices to mark the attendances of student and employees accurately. The face recognition performance with ‘Pehchan’ improves with its use as a layer in a deep neural network, which yields a robust classification for heterogeneous face recognition with respect to the state-of-the art methods.

Motivation

  • It is often seen in colleges, taking and tracking attendance of students and professors becomes a time-taking job using the traditional approach. Also, it isn't very accurate as anyone can tamper the data. To save this valuable time and effort, this system is developed.

  • To further reduce the time spent to mark attendance, a multiple face recognition system comes to play which not only marks attendance in a classroom or office space but also provides the data in such a format that can be used by organisations to display it on their web portal.
